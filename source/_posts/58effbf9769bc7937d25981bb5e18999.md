<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">我很喜欢かなえ前辈说的台词。</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">每次演唱会都会竖起耳朵听那些有变化的台词。听到和彩排不一样的语气时，心跳都会漏掉一拍。</span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">第11张单曲，能和她分到同样的歌词部分真的好开心好开心。无论是和かなえ前辈对称位，还是歌词分在一起的曲子，都让我很高兴。</span></p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 9px 0px 8px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"><div class="nananiji-img-placeholder"></div></span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">虽然是一首直抵心扉的歌，但《命の続き》成了我非常重要的曲子。</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">能和かなえ前辈有类似轮唱独唱的部分真的太开心了！</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">在曲子里我也非常喜欢那部分的旋律，真的非常非常开心呀。</span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">1段副歌时要在かなえ前辈之后说台词，所以我总是站在かなえ前辈身后坐立不安地等待着。能承接かなえ前辈的台词再说出自己的台词，这种经验让我很幸福。</span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">休息日和家人去了开运景点，紧接着歌词分配表就发来了，正好和かなえ前辈在一起！</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">当时还高兴地喊着“开运了〜！！！”</span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">有好多曲子都会让我想起かなえ前辈。</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">今后我也想怀揣着这些回忆，珍惜从她那里获得的一切，继续歌唱、继续跳舞</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">能一起站在舞台上真的太幸福了。</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">完全没有必要去忘记！</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">我会带着这些回忆继续努力的〜！！！</span><span class="s4">😌</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">这样一想，我才发现与かなえ前辈那些珍贵的回忆一直都在我身边。</span></p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 9px 0px 8px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"><div class="nananiji-img-placeholder"></div></span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">我们曾经都是“连裤袜组”的。</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">记得听说可能要穿连裤袜的时候，我还问过かなえ前辈“会很热吗??”。</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">结果就像かなえ前辈说的那样，其实没啥差别。</span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">每次穿演出服时，看到原本准备了两双的连裤袜现在只剩下一双了，就觉得好寂寞</span><span class="s4">😔</span></p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 9px 0px 8px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"><div class="nananiji-img-placeholder"></div></span></p>
<p class="p2" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; min-height: 22px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">和かなえ前辈拍照时总是很紧张，整个人都僵住了。特别是《声优Grand Prix》里那个拥抱的镜头。</span></p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 9px 0px 8px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"><div class="nananiji-img-placeholder"></div></span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">因为太紧张了，几乎没什么记忆。</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">最近终于变得能主动抱上去了</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">7月的时候只要有空隙我好像经常粘上去抱抱。</span></p>
<p class="p3" style="margin: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s3">变成了粘人精呢</span></p>
<p class="p4" style="margin: 9px 0px 8px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;"><span class="s2"><div class="nananiji-img-placeholder"></div></span></p>
